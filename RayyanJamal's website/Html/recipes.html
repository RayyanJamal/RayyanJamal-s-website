Madras Curry Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup chopped green peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp. curry powder
  • 1-1/2 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 can (14-1/2 oz.) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. dried thyme leaves
  • 1/8 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/2 lb. (8 oz.) VELVEETA, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 3 cups cooked long-grain white rice

Method

  1. Melt butter in large saucepan on medium heat. Add green peppers, onions, garlic and curry powder; cook and stir until vegetables are crisp-tender. Add chicken; cook and stir 5 min. or until no longer pink.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, lemon juice, thyme and black pepper; simmer on low heat 5 min. or until chicken is done, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add VELVEETA; cook until melted, stirring frequently. Serve over rice.

Quick Chicken Teriyaki

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Lite Zesty Italian Dressing
  • 1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1-1/3 cups water
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 cups frozen broccoli florets
  • 1-1/2 cups instant white rice, uncooked

Method

  1. Heat dressing in large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook and stir 5 to 7 min. or until done.
  2. Add water, teriyaki sauce and garlic powder; stir. Bring to boil.
  3. Stir in broccoli and rice; cover. Cook on low heat 5 min. Remove from heat. Let stand, covered, 5 min. Fluff with fork.

Taco Salad Made Over

Ingredients

  • 4 flour tortillas (8 inch)
  • 3/4 lb. extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 Tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 cup rinsed canned kidney beans
  • 1/2 cup TACO BELL® Thick & Chunky Salsa
  • 4 cups packed torn mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup KRAFT 2% Milk Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp. KRAFT Lite Ranch Dressing

Method

  1. Heat oven to 425°F.
  2. Crumple 4 sheets of foil to make 4 (3-inch) balls; place on baking sheet. Top each with 1 tortilla; spray with cooking spray. Bake 6 to 8 min. or until tortillas are golden brown. (Tortillas will drape over balls as they bake to form shells.)
  3. Meanwhile, brown meat with chili powder in large nonstick skillet. Stir in beans and salsa; cook 3 to 5 min. or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
  4. Fill tortilla shells with salad greens, meat mixture, cheese and tomatoes. Drizzle with dressing.

Simple Southern-Style 'Unfried' Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (4 lb.), cut up
  • 1/2 cup KRAFT Lite Ranch Dressing
  • 1 pkt. SHAKE 'N BAKE Extra Crispy Seasoned Coating Mix

Method

  1. Remove skin from all chicken pieces except wings. Place chicken in large resealable plastic bag. Add dressing. Seal bag; turn to evenly coat chicken with dressing. Refrigerate 30 min. to marinate.
  2. Heat oven to 400°F. Place coating mix in pie plate or shallow dish. Dip chicken in coating mix, turning to evenly coat both sides of each piece. Place in single layer on baking sheet. Sprinkle with any remaining coating mix. Discard bag and marinade.
  3. Bake 40 to 45 min. or until chicken is done (165°F).

Mediterranean Mozza-Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 small boneless skinless chicken breasts (1 lb.)
  • 1 red onion, cut lengthwise in half, then sliced crosswise
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 cup KRAFT Tuscan House Italian Dressing
  • 1/2 cup halved grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup pitted black olives
  • 1 cup KRAFT 2% Milk Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Method

  1. Heat large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken; cover skillet with lid. Cook 5 to 7 min. on each side or until chicken is done (165ºF), adding onions and zucchini after 5 min.
  2. Add dressing, tomatoes and olives; cover. Cook on medium-low heat 2 min. or until heated through; stir.
  3. Top with cheese; cook, covered, 2 min. or until melted.

Easy Italian Baked Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. extra-lean ground beef
  • 3 cups whole grain penne pasta, cooked
  • 1 jar (24 oz.) CLASSICO Tomato and Basil Pasta Sauce
  • 1/3 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese, divided
  • 1-1/2 cups KRAFT 2% Milk Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Method

  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Brown meat in large skillet; drain. Return to skillet. Add pasta, pasta sauce and half the Parmesan; mix well.
  3. Spoon into 13x9-inch baking dish sprayed with cooking spray; top with remaining cheeses.
  4. Bake 20 min. or until heated through.
